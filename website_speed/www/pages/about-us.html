About Us

Speed ​​is a leading Yemeni company in the field of trading and shipping cars and spare parts, with more than 19 years of experience in the field.

Speed ​​is a leading Yemeni company in the field of trading and shipping cars and spare parts, with more than 19 years of experience in this field. The company is characterized by providing innovative shipping solutions that outperform its competitors, and relies on closed and covered cars to protect cars during the shipping process. Speed ​​also provides services such as buying cars from auctions, examining and packaging them, in addition to providing spare parts and distinguished international shipping services. The company is keen to constantly develop its services to meet the requirements of its customers, and to achieve distinguished successes to become one of the leading companies in the field of freight and logistics services in the automotive world.

Transportation service in Europe

Our company has been dealing with the acquisition and transport of vehicles from the US since 2015. Since then, we have managed to import over the thousound cars from different parts of North America. Apart from passenger cars, we also import motorcycles, quads and water scooters. We provide services for both individual clients and traders, as well as for wholesale customers. Thanks to our continuous cooperation with foreign companies, we can guarantee the best negotiated freight and land transport rates. In Speed, we bet on transparency and ease of purchase, which is why our transport rates are known before making a bid. On our website, you will find a calculator calculating the total transaction cost, and thanks to this, clients do not incur any additional hidden costs.
