A few words about Speed

We specialize in acquiring and transporting vehicles from the United States since 2015.

Transportation service in Europe

Our company has been dealing with the acquisition and transport of vehicles from the US since 2015. Since then, we have managed to import over the thousound cars from different parts of North America. Apart from passenger cars, we also import motorcycles, quads and water scooters. We provide services for both individual clients and traders, as well as for wholesale customers. Thanks to our continuous cooperation with foreign companies, we can guarantee the best negotiated freight and land transport rates. In BidCars, we bet on transparency and ease of purchase, which is why our transport rates are known before making a bid. On our website, you will find a calculator calculating the total transaction cost, and thanks to this, clients do not incur any additional hidden costs.
